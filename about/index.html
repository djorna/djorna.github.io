Hi, I’m David Jorna. I plan to use this site to share my ideas, programming projects, and technical knowledge, in the hope that someone finds it to be helpful. Conventional wisdom says that you should keep your blog topics focused on a niche in order to gain traction, but I will likely cover a variety of topics. Some of my interests include artificial intelligence, computer vision, education, philosophy, science, and technology, so you can expect content along those lines.
